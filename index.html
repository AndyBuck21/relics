Welcome to Relics of Ecstasy

Relics signify the development of character from a spiritual stand point. By submerging each in different colors, our belief in the highest level of pleasure and satisfaction lying in the best, most unique version of ourselves in the real and virtual world [ECSTAVERSE] is demonstrated..

You are a Relic of Ecstasy because each character created was created with you in mind. Each character holds a link to the past world (ancestral) and present world we live in, the people we model after and who really are.

In this space, all that matters is finding yourself as the goal is to create a metaverse, #ectasverse where EVERYONE is welcome and can be themselves.

Roadmap

First Mile

    The journey begins with no brakes. Social media campaigns, website launch and a community with no discord is developed on different platform.

Second Mile

    Assign roles to relics via website and discord. Host giveaways for active Twitter participants in our space. Understanding the relics is foremost in this race.

Third Mile

    The relics of official public mint is revealed. Collectors now see what's in store; a relic is only as special as its holder

Fourth Mile

    Merch items with unlockable NFT content are released. This will herald the dawn of the Relics of Optical Ecstasy (ROE) fashion line. "Ecstasick" argumented reality filters are developed for public release.

Fifth Mile

    10% of primary sales will be donated to a non-profit organization towards improving mental institutions and patients

Sixth Mile

    Initiate second wave;
    "What's next for Relics of Ecstasy?"
    We wind up in the Metaverse, expoloring plans to build the Ecstaverse; a place for everyone and no one.

Seventh Mile

    The podcast takeover will happen after a poll on our discord channel point to a preferred host.
